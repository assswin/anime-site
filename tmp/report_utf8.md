Performance Improvements</strong></h1><h2><strong>4.1 Video Loading Strategy</strong></h2><p>Currently all character videos are likely loaded upfront. With 10 MP4 files, this can mean 100ΓÇô500MB of network requests on page load. Implement intersection-observer-based loading:</p><ul><li>Add preload="none" on all video elements by default</li><li>Switch to preload="metadata" when card enters viewport (within 1 card distance)</li><li>Only call video.load() and video.play() when card becomes active</li><li>Pause and unload videos that are more than 2 cards away</li></ul><p>Expected impact: 80ΓÇô90% reduction in initial network payload, faster Time to Interactive.</p><h2><strong>4.2 Particle Count Reduction</strong></h2><p>The WeatherSystem and FloatingParticles components generate between 8 and 50 animated elements simultaneously.
